Privacy Policy

Secure Gmail Sender

Last updated: 07/11/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Secure Gmail Sender collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our application.

1. Data we collect

  • Account information: Email address and the OAuth tokens necessary to access Gmail (access tokens & refresh tokens).
  • Message metadata: Subject, recipients, and delivery status when you use the service.

2. How we use your data

We use the data to perform the service you request: sending email on your behalf via the Gmail API. We do not read or store email content longer than necessary for sending (unless you opt in to message logging).

3. Token storage & retention

Refresh tokens are stored encrypted in our database. You may revoke access at any time via the Google Account settings or by contacting us at makaimurphy007@gmail.com. We will delete your stored tokens within 30 days of a deletion request.

4. Third-party services

We do not share your information with third parties except where required to operate the service (e.g., cloud hosting and encrypted storage providers). Any third-party services we use adhere to industry-standard security practices.

5. Security

We follow best practices: HTTPS-only communication, encrypted token storage, periodic security reviews, and least-privilege OAuth scopes.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact: makaimurphy007@gmail.com
